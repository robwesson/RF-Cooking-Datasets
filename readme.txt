These datasets are the result of simulating heating patterns across phase and frequency 
for a microwave oven cavity with two frequency bands and two types of load.

Data is the result of simulation with CST Microwave Studio 
License is the property of TE Connectivity).

The work is part of the deliverable for Robin Wessons Masters Dissertation 
in Software Engineering at the University of Liverpool (Online Program)

These datasets remain the intellectual property of Robin Wesson and the University of Liverpool.
They are shared in the hope that the data will support further research into the interesting topic of solid state RF heating quality.

Please acknowledge the source of the data in any work you do with this data, and please also share it back!
